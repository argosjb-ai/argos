Argos is designed for personal productivity — managing your communications, calendar, and information using AI assistance. You may use Argos for any lawful personal purpose consistent with these Terms.

1. Harmful or Illegal Content

You must not use Argos to generate, distribute, or facilitate:

  • Content that is illegal under applicable law

2. Misuse of AI Capabilities

You must not:

  • Attempt to manipulate, jailbreak, or circumvent Argos's safety guidelines or those of the underlying AI model (Anthropic's Claude)

3. Unauthorised Data Access

You must not use Argos to:

  • Access Gmail or Google Calendar accounts that you do not own or have explicit authorisation to access

4. Service Integrity

You must not:

  • Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or tamper with Argos or its underlying infrastructure
  • Use automated tools to interact with Argos in a way that places unreasonable load on our systems or Anthropic's API
